Room-by-Room Assessment

Tap a category to open it. Your progress saves automatically. Severity scale: Safe → Minor → Moderate → High → Critical. Any CRITICAL finding flags the home for immediate safety attention.

    Modification Plan

    Every finding ranked Minor or higher appears here grouped by severity. Add a cost estimate and suggested timeline. Items marked CRITICAL trigger an immediate-action flag in both reports.

    Home Safety Score

    Score is calculated from CSA B652:23 feature weightings. A score of 100 = no safety concerns identified. A score below 60 indicates multiple high-severity barriers that warrant attention for aging in place.

    About This Tool

    Senior Home Safety Assessment Tool v1
    Built for Carl Christie, Novo Industries Inc., for the Senior Home Safety service line. Uses CSA/ASC B652:23 Accessible Dwellings as the technical reference. This tool produces assessment data and dual-audience draft reports. It is not a diagnostic medical instrument; findings support but do not replace clinical judgment from an Occupational Therapist or physician.

    Data is stored only in this browser's local storage. No data leaves your device. If you clear your browser data or switch devices without exporting a backup, your assessment data will be lost.

    A note on Bluetooth laser measures: Browser-based web apps cannot connect directly to Bluetooth laser measures on iPad or iPhone (Apple has not implemented Web Bluetooth). The recommended workflow is to use the Bosch MeasureOn app natively on your phone or iPad, then enter measurements manually into this tool. Each measurement field accepts numeric input with unit hints.
