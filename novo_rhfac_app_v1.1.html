    About This Tool

    Novo RHFAC Assessment Tool v1.1
    Built for Carl Christie, Novo Industries Inc. Uses RHFAC v4.0 Rating Survey methodology and CSA/ASC B651:23 standards. This tool produces assessment data and draft reports with a selectable report type (Municipal includes Next Dollar Impact; Private Sector, Healthcare, Educational, and General omit the capital pathway section). It does not submit to the RHFAC Registry. Registry submission remains a manual step through registry.rickhansen.com.

    Data is stored only in this browser's local storage. No data leaves your device. If you clear your browser data or switch devices without exporting a backup, your assessment data will be lost.
